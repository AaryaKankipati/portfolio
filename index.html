About Me

I completed my Undergraduation in the field of Electronics and communication Engineering at Vignan's Foundation for Science, Research and Technology in the year of 2020.

Projects

    • Vignan's Foundation for Science, Research and Technology, India (August,2018-December,2019)
      • This project is mainly works to detect gas leak and can interface control system so a process can be automatically shut down.This type of device is important because there are many gases that can be harmful to organic life, such as humans or animals.
